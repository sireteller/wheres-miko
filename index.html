Miko's Big Day Out

This is Miko. He has gone out all on his own and is now hiding in all sorts of likely and unlikely locations. Your task is to find him in 5 of these locations and click on him to tell him to come home.

When you hover over Miko in the scene, the cursor will turn into a pointer . Makes it easier to comb through the image.

Confident in your 'Where's Waldo' abilities? In hard mode, the levels are the same, but the cursor won't turn into a pointer when you hover over Miko.
